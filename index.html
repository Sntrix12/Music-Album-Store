Frequently Asked Questions

How do I redeem my Gift Card?

Gift cards can be redeemed by entering the code during checkout or adding the balance to your SoundVault wallet in your account settings.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes! Our subscriptions are entirely commitment-free. You can cancel your monthly or yearly plan whenever you want through your account dashboard.

Can I pick up my albums at an offline store?

Yes! You can order online and choose to pick up your physical albums and gift cards at any of our offline store locations for added convenience.

Our Story

Every great venture starts with a shared playlist. For us—Mahi, Shreeya, and Vanshika—music has always been more than just background noise; it’s the heartbeat of our friendship and the blueprint for our biggest dreams.

What began as late-night debates over tracklists and hunt-downs for rare pressings eventually turned into a mission: to create a space where music lovers can find albums that truly resonate. We believe that an album is a time capsule—a physical piece of art that connects the artist's vision to your own personal journey.

Why We’re Here

We didn't just want to build a store; we wanted to build a bridge between legendary sounds and the people who live for them.

The Curated Touch: We don’t just "stock" music. From timeless classics to the newest underground releases, every album in our collection is hand-picked because it moved us.

For the Fans, By the Fans: We know the thrill of unsealing a fresh record or finally holding a physical copy of that one album that changed everything for you.

A Shared Dream: This website is the result of three friends deciding that the music we love deserves to be heard, held, and celebrated.

Meet the Team

We’re just three friends who turned a shared obsession into a reality. Whether you're looking for the warmth of analog vinyl or the pristine clarity of a limited-edition CD, we’re here to help you find the next great soundtrack to your life.

Welcome to the family. Let’s keep the music playing.
